Keshav Associates was founded on 6th September 2019 by Parshotam Sharma, a LIC Senior Insurance Advisor. Driven by a passion to make official services more accessible and customer-friendly, Parshotam created a space that offers personalized support for various administrative and documentation needs.

Over the years, we have assisted countless individuals with everything from LIC policy corrections and new applications to passport forms and voter ID cards. We believe in empowering our clients with knowledge, support, and solutions. Our center also offers digital services including instant passport-size photos, online form filling, and insurance for vehicles.

With a wide range of services under one roof and a strong reputation built on trust and efficiency, Keshav Associates continues to be the go-to hub for everyday essential services.
